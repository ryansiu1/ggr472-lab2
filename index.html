Architecture and Art

Visualizing Using MapBox Studio


In our previous website, we could only look at the GeoJSON code for the GTA's City Halls... what use is that? This time around, we're able to integrate a webmap into our website and visualize the locations and distribution of the architectural wonders!

In this exercise, we are also going to explore some other areas in the GTA where you can appreciate some art! On the map to the left, we have our city hall buildings symbolized in blue. An assortment of public work art throughout the city are symbolized in yellow.
